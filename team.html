Introducing Team Phoenix from IIT Patna! We're a tight-knit bunch of students who love robotics and creativity. With a shared interest in technology and solving problems, we've teamed up to take on the exciting Roboccon competition. Our diverse skills create a dynamic group that's all about exploring new ideas and pushing the boundaries of engineering. We're on a journey of learning, teamwork, and showcasing our robot's skills on the Roboccon stage. As Team Phoenix, we are committed to competing, learning, growing, and having a great time while doing what we love. Stay tuned as we work tirelessly, putting our minds and machines to the test, and showcase our passion for robotics on the grand stage of Robocon.
