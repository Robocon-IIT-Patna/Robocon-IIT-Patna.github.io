What We Do

Workshops

Robocon and the allied clubs of IIT Patna, including the Tinkering Lab, are committed to promoting knowledge-sharing and skill development. They organize regular workshops covering a diverse range of topics, featuring engaging demonstrations of our latest projects to inspire and empower participants.

Robocon National Finals

The prestigious National Finals of Robocon are jointly hosted by Doordarshan and IIT Delhi, and the electrifying event is broadcasted live on DD Bharti, captivating audiences across the nation.

Design and simulate

We excel in creating intricate 3D models and conducting realistic robot simulations as a crucial part of our initial ideation process. Our dedication to leveraging cutting-edge technology enables us to craft innovative solutions with precision and vision.

App and Web Dev

We excel in web and app development, crafting cutting-edge solutions to meet diverse needs that include monitoring and control of our latest instruments.

Prototype and Test Wacky Mechanisms

Building a robot every year involves trying out wild mechanisms, most don't work but hey, that's real Engineering

ML OPS

We deploy exerimental pre-trained and custom made models for novel applications in problem solving.

Circuit Design

Designing and Optimizing our PCB's as per requirements each year, focussing on upgradability of PCB's created.

What is ABU Robocon

Asian Broadcasters' Union Robocon is the largest and oldest pan-asian robotics challenge that has hundreds of teams over many asian countries.

Problem Statement

Every year we recieve a problem statement which is unique.

Brainstorm

We come up with novel concepts in order to solve the given problem. A lot of headscratching and wild ideas are involved.

The Stages

We commence with design and demo rounds and finally the Qualified teams head for the National finals in New Delhi.

Dassault Systemes

Dassault Systèmes is a French multinational software corporation that develops software for 3D product design, simulation, manufacturing, and other 3D related products. The company is headquartered in Vélizy-Villacoublay, France, and has over 290,000 customers in 140 countries. Dassault Systèmes' 3D modeling software is used by a wide range of industries, including aerospace, automotive, consumer goods, manufacturing, and healthcare. The software allows users to create, visualize, and simulate 3D models of products, which can be used for a variety of purposes, such as design, testing, and manufacturing.

Technology and Innovation Hub, IIT Patna

TIH IIT Patna stands for Technology Incubation Hub IIT Patna. It is a non-profit company that was set up by IIT Patna in 2017 under the National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber Physical Systems (NMICPS) of the Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India. The main objective of TIH IIT Patna is to promote entrepreneurship and innovation in the field of cyber physical systems. It does this by providing incubation, hand-holding, go-to-market strategy, and funding to early-stage startups.

Tech Analogy

Tech Analogy is a company that provides STEM education to individuals of all ages, regardless of their location or social standing. They offer a variety of courses and workshops on topics such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, robotics, and cybersecurity. Tech Analogy's mentorship program is designed to help students develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in STEM careers. The program pairs students with experienced professionals who can provide guidance and support.

TechProLabz

Techprolabz is a pioneer in the field of Robotics. It is an organisation that invests in creating new innovations, and executing them to bring out new technology. Today the world is craving for new "Kalams" and Einsteins and so. Techprolabz is trying hard to fill that gap and we act as a bridge to provide kids the platform that will make themselves land in between top tech-lovers.
